My Autobiography

By Michelle Anne A. De Tomas

Introduction

Hello! My name is Michelle Anne A. De Tomas, and this is my autobiography. I was born on October 07, 2003 in Marikina City. Throughout my life, I have experienced many events that have shaped who I am today.

Early Life

I spent my childhood in Nangka, Marikina City. Growing up, I enjoyed playing online games such as Mobile Legends and Call of Duty Mobile. My family played a big role in shaping my values and personality.

Education

I attended Nangka Elementary School for my early education and later went to Marikina Polytechnic College where I studied Bachelor of Industrial Technology Major in Civil Technology. These experiences helped me grow intellectually and socially.

Career

My professional journey began at Niceman Incorporated. Over the years, I’ve worked on projects related to Civil Technology. I take pride in my achievements and continue to pursue growth in my career.

Personal Life

Outside of work, I enjoy reading books and walking around in parks. I believe in living life with balance—spending time with family, friends, and doing what I love.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I hope to live my dream life like financially, emotionally, and physically stable. I am passionate about continuous learning and making a positive impact in my community.
